# Fabric Capacities: From Mechanics to Strategy

**Audience:** Fabric and capacity administrators, analytics team leaders

## Abstract

Capacities underpin everything in Fabric, yet for many teams they remain a black box. You're getting throttled - but why? You're migrating from Premium to Fabric - will your workloads behave the same? You suspect you're overpaying - but how would you even tell?

This session gives you the understanding to answer these questions yourself. We'll start with how capacities actually work - bursting, smoothing, how usage gets charged - then explore the avenues available for managing and optimizing your capacity. Using the Capacity Metrics App, we'll look at how to interpret what's happening and what levers you can pull.

Whether you're troubleshooting performance issues, planning a migration, or just want to finally understand what's going on under the hood - you'll leave with a clearer picture of how to navigate your capacity environment.

## Target Audience

- Fabric capacity administrators
- Fabric tenant administrators
- Analytics team leaders managing capacity
- IT leaders responsible for Fabric infrastructure
- Content creators wanting to understand the platform

## Key Takeaways

1. Understanding how Fabric capacities work: bursting, smoothing, and consumption mechanics
2. How to interpret the Capacity Metrics App and diagnose performance issues
3. Practical approaches to capacity optimization and cost management
4. Migration considerations from Premium to Fabric capacities
5. What levers you can pull to manage capacity behavior
